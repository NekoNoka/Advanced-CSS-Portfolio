Our World Of Pixels Addon

Our World Of Pixels

Our world of pixels is a website utilizes the collective efforts of anyone joining to draw whatever they want, or they can act selfishly and ruin other peoples works.
The original purpose of my addon is to assist artists in the environment of our world of pixels so they can draw without being griefed on.
As time went on I've added many more features than just to assist artists from being griefed on such as
  • line gradient
  • copy&paste
  • paste patterns
  • changing the draw size
    • and much more

Pixel Manager

The pixel manager will simply handle pixels being placed, whether someone griefs your work or you decide to paste something the pixel manager will place the pixels down automatically.

Pasting Tool

This tool lets the user draw a picture by uploading a png to the website, the pixel manager will automatically place the pixels.
